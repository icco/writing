Second week complete! This post is a little later than I'd like, but here we are. Up front, the only music I published this week was [one song in drifts](https://subalpinedrift.bandcamp.com/track/2026-04-21-blocks). I did finish two more songs and make progress on another though, so I'm making good headway.

At the beginning of the week, I was spending a lot of time thinking about "what's the point". That thought was a bit in a depressed way, but mostly in a "what am I trying to say" or a "why am I making this art" way. I haven't really come up with a good answer, beyond I want to share what I'm creating... which is something. Something I've mentioned to people in person, and possibly on this blog, I'm not sure, is I struggle with the fact that I am bad at this. My two creative outlets for so long have been coding and writing. When I wanted to stretch a creative muscle, that's what I did. I'll write in a notebook or code up some dumb little app (it's why I was able to [archive 200+ Github repos last week](/posts/772) or why I have a total of [460 repos on Github](https://github.com/icco/)). It's not good writing or good code, but I can write it and I can properly traverse a flow from "idea" to "I wonder if that's possible" to "oh look, a basic thing".

An example of doing this happened yesterday, which I'll technically put in Week Three territory but since I was late on this post, I guess I'll shove in here. I wrote a little Discord bot. I took [timball/Careen](https://github.com/timball/Careen),[song.link](https://odesli.co/), and a few other ideas (including past discord bots), shoved it into [Cursor](https://cursor.com/), made some tweaks, and ended up with [linkbot](https://linkbot.natwelch.com/). I wanted it because I am debating moving off of Spotify, and wanted something to genericise links I would post in music channels for communities I am in. It took about five hours to go form "idea" to "oh this works".

Music has not been that way. I am too new. I struggle at every step.

 - With idea generation, I don't know what's possible so I can't doodle as easily as I can writing or with code.
 - With execution & crafting, I struggle to turn an idea into something finished.
 - With finishing, I don't know what's good, so I don't know when to stop.

Coming to terms with that being OK has been a journey these last two weeks. And in good news: I think I am finally accepting that. Friday I feel like I had a bit of a break through. I listened to all of the sounds I have recorded over the last two weeks. Random nature sounds. Bleeps. Bloops. Songs. Me singing about bugs. A lot was trash or useless, but I could see progress, and there was stuff that I liked. Things that I created where I was like, oh hey, I made that and I like it.

Things that helped me get there, beyond just spending time making music, included:

- [An interview with Flying Lotus](https://www.musicradar.com/artists/there-was-this-guy-who-lived-in-the-dorm-he-was-a-great-friend-and-an-awesome-artist-and-he-actually-made-cool-music-with-the-computer-and-it-was-like-wait-you-can-do-that-flying-lotus-on-early-musical-adventures-ai-and-more) where he talks about how some people prefer his music from when he knew nothing.
- [Alan Tudyk Eats His Last Meal](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sM0pzP4280U), because of his descriptions of how not every artist's life turns out well.
- [Bâan](https://youtu.be/V-P3HWXGYm4?si=AvrfUI-UgzM0-pzs) and [the making of documentary](https://youtu.be/4gO00P13hG0?si=HOATzXuuRTsN-T_9). Both are very moving stories and was interesting to think about control and design.

I also consumed [a lot of music](https://www.last.fm/user/icco/library?from=2026-04-17&to=2026-04-27). Some of the albums that stuck out to me included:

- [Trying Times by James Blake](https://open.spotify.com/album/16tV8wTfqPyA6Cu5dwJJPc?si=CsXLPt_yTg64O46DdJApnQ)
- [Time Heals Everything by Blu & Exile](https://open.spotify.com/album/4Ndz4qFpmRfm6Jx4xiLY2f?si=KUgwg08QSVacG22BC_x7jA)
- [The Great Divide by Noah Kahan](https://open.spotify.com/album/0TSynllAnze7KMhv6J7W18?si=WPWxZONESESIwWTbZnuJCA)
- [Ghosts I-IV by Nine Inch Nails](https://open.spotify.com/album/0lOn8nKk4dzzRfnCCCRbwp?si=YEQRkJB2R_6_yp3ffsbqgA)


I continued my distractions of writing code, fixing up [robot.villas](https://robot.villas) more, building another midi [script for norns: pauper](https://github.com/icco/pauper), and cleaning up my personal website and this blog.

I also watched a lot of TV... more than I'd like to admit. I think because I was depressed, and we had a cold snap, so I stayed inside a bit more.

Finally some photos of my time this week are below. I did go on a few more hikes, and I did some recording in various places around the town and in [Prince Gallitzin State Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_Gallitzin_State_Park).
