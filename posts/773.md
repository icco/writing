Second week complete! I have been spending a lot of time thinking about "what's the point". A bit in a depressed way but mostly in a "what am I trying to say" or a "why am I making this art" way. I haven't really come up with a good answer, beyond I want to share what I'm creating... which is something.

We had a weird cold snap here, which I stayed inside for more of this week than last, which I think triggered some of my depression a bit.
