I was talking to [my mom](https://lydiadehn.com), and she wanted some examples of the kind of music that I am trying to make. To do this, I wanted to pull together three example albums for her to listen to. It's worth noting that the ambient and experimental genres are huge. Just check out [The Best Experimental Music on Bandcamp, April 2026](https://daily.bandcamp.com/best-experimental/the-best-experimental-music-on-bandcamp-april-2026) or [The Best Ambient Music on Bandcamp, March 2026](https://daily.bandcamp.com/best-ambient/the-best-ambient-music-on-bandcamp-march-2026). Each of the albums are pretty different. Some commonalities, but in general very unique.

All of this to say, I picked three things here, but they do not summarize the genres.

## 1. hypervisibility by then

https://tehn.bandcamp.com/album/hypervisibility

This is a relatively new album that I love a lot. tehn, aka [Brian Crabtree](https://nnnnnnnn.co/), is the designer and creator of [Monome](https://monome.org/) and also just an incredible artist. I've been following his work since around 2018, and it has always inspired me in some way.

## 2. Dani Derks + Jonathan Snyder (220924 @ luck dragon)

https://youtu.be/yi5bkzskNHU?si=48IRp81jzrH1Xszq

Related, Brian and Kelly Crabtree also operate [luck dragon](https://luckdragon.space/) in Delhi, NY. It's an art store and general space. I attended a weekend workshop there in 2022 run by [Dani Derks](https://web.archive.org/web/20260313192802/https://dndrks.com/) and [Jonathan Snyder](https://www.instagram.com/jaseknighter/). This performance was in the evening and I really enjoyed it. My only complaint is I don't think the recording properly captures Jonathan's performance in all of its details well. I remember lots of breathing sounds, which I don't hear as much on the youtube.

## 3. Selected Jambient Works Vol. 1 by Cowboy Sadness

https://cowboysadness.bandcamp.com/album/selected-jambient-works-vol-1

I picked this because it's a local band. They are from Kingston, NY. It's more fast paced then what I usually want to make, but I do like it a lot.
